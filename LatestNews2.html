  • Home
  • LatestNews2

The objectives of “ACM INTER COLLEGE WEB DESIGNING CONTEST” are as follows: -

 To give Students the know the standards of the expectations in the society for web designing.
 To enhance designing ability and help students in showcasing themselves in a group of talented people.
 To help identify problematic areas where the student needs to improve so that he/she can tackle the interviews with ease
 To know new technologies to work with it and learn about various technologies available in the market

2. Theme of the Event.

Web Designing is one of the most easy and important skill that a student can have in his/her B.Tech life. Web Designing decides the branding of a company and can be pivotal for the operations. With more and more work becoming cloud based, there is inherent need for designing and creation of clean and usable UI for every application. With ever increasing number of frameworks for development and the need for students with basic idea on how and what of web has become a striking requirement for every company. Going through this contest provides the students with ability to work on new technologies, network and learn new things.
